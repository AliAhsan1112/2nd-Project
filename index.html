Mini Seekh Kabab

Ingredients:

Mince (beef) ½ kg

Ginger 1 piece

Egg 1

Green chilies 2

Garlic cloves 4 bulb

Onions (fried) 1 cup

Red chili powder 2 tsp

Desiccated coconut 1 tbsp

All spice powder ½ tsp

Mace ½ tsp

Salt ½ tsp

Chicken cube 1

Oil for frying

Method:

In a chopper add beef mince, green chilies, garlic cloves, ginger, desiccated coconut, fried onions, all spice powder, mace, red chili powder, chicken cube and salt. Grind it properly. Then add egg and mix well. Now make mini seekh kababs. Then heat oil and fry the seekh kababs. Serve hot.

Whether it a barbecue night with friends or family, Eid festivities or a dinner at your favourite desi restaurant, it feels incomplete with one of the most popular Pakistani dish, the juicy beef seekh kabab.

Beef seekh kabab are a type of kebab, that are extremely popular in thhe subcontinent, made with spiced minced or ground meat. Even though they are commonly made with minced beef there are quite a few variations and are now made in lamb and chicken as well. The minced meat is then formed into cylinders on skewers and grilled. It is typically cooked on a mangal or barbecue, or in a tandoor.
